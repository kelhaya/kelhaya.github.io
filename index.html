Bio-polymer development at Fraunhofer Institute

In the department for bio-polymers of the Fraunhofer Institute for applied polymers, I was responsible for the project ‘Development of starch-based flame-retardants, modified with phosphorus-nitrogen synergy systems in bio plastics’. I developed the material from the initial concept, to the large-scale modelling.
