MSc. curriculum for University of Hamburg

For a sustainable circular economy, it is important to use any resource to the fullest. Even the waste or byproduct of one industry can be a resource for another. One of such byproducts is lignin which is waste material from the paper industry. In this project, I worked as a material engineer to develop bio-based plastics from lignin for both 3D printing and injection moulding. Moreover, I designed the curriculum and lecture material for the international MSc. course "Introduction to Polymers" for University of Hamburg. This includes interactive e-learning material for online classes during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The topics of the curriculum include:

  • Introduction to polymer material science
  • Polymer structure property relations
  • Individual Polymers
  • Polymers additives
  • Polymer manufacturing processes
  • Polymer testing and characterization
  • Polymer degradation
